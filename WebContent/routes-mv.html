Maple Valley Holiday Engine Routes

These are the Maple Valley routes for the 2020 Jerry Woods Holiday Engine. The listed times below are approximate. You can use the Track Santa link to follow the engines on the nights listed below.

If you are a part of a youth organization, i.e. scouts or a sports team, and would like to help us with the Holiday Engine as we collect food in local neighborhoods, please reach out to us at signup@holidayengine.org.

See all of the neighborhoods covered in Maple Valley on one page.
